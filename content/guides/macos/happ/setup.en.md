## Adding a Subscription Link
1. Open the bot and use the service management button to get your subscription link. Then copy the link.

<img src="/assets/macos/happ/1.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<img src="/assets/macos/happ/2.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. The first time you open Happ, a window like the one below will appear. Paste your link into the center box.

<img src="/assets/macos/happ/3.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

3. If successful, the new servers will be added to your server list.

<img src="/assets/macos/happ/4.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">

<br>

4. Select the server you want, then click the large button. Before that, make sure TUN is enabled as shown in the image.
<img src="/assets/macos/happ/5.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

5. You may be asked for your MacBook password to connect to the VPN. Enter your password, then click OK.
<img src="/assets/macos/happ/6.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

Congratulations, you are connected to the free internet! :)

:::tip

**✅ Tip**
If you click the connect button but the VPN does not turn on, restart your MacBook once to fix the issue.
:::

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**
1. Do not keep another VPN connected while adding the update link.

2. Make sure your device's time and date are set to automatic.

3. Do not copy any extra text with your subscription link. Copy only the subscription link.
:::
