## Sorting Servers by Ping

1. In the Happ app, click the highlighted area shown in the image and wait a moment.

<img src="/assets/macos/happ/8.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>


2. Server pings will appear one by one next to each server name. Connect to the server with the lowest ping.

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while sorting.
:::
