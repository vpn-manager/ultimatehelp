## Sorting Servers by Ping

1. In the Streisand app, double-click or right-click the VPN section until the options appear.

<img src="/assets/macos/streisand/10.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. Click Latency to run a ping test for the server. You can also click Auto Connect to automatically connect to the best server.

<img src="/assets/macos/streisand/7.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

3. Set SORT BY: to PING so the servers are sorted from the lowest ping.


:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while sorting.
:::
