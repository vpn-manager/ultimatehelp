## Updating the Subscription Link

1. In the Streisand app, press and hold the VPN section for a few seconds until the options appear.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/17.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. Tap Update to update the servers.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/13.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

3. If successful, the message below will appear at the top of the screen.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/3.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while sorting.

:::
