## Sorting Servers by Ping

1. In the Streisand app, press and hold the VPN section for a few seconds until the options appear.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/17.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. Tap Latency to run a ping test for the server.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/12.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

3. You can also tap Auto connect to automatically connect to the best server.

<img src="/assets/ios/streisand/11.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

4. Set SORT BY: to PING so the servers are sorted from the lowest ping.


:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while sorting.
:::
