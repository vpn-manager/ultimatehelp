[🛒 Download from App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/happ-proxy-utility/id6504287215)

## Adding a Subscription Link
1. Open the bot and use the service management button to get your subscription link. Then copy the link.

<img src="/assets/ios/happ/1.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<img src="/assets/ios/happ/3.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. Open the Happ app, tap + in the top-right corner, then select Import from clipboard to add the servers.

<img src="/assets/ios/happ/4.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">


<img src="/assets/ios/happ/5.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

3. If successful, the new servers will be added to your server list.

<img src="/assets/ios/happ/10.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">

<br>

4. Select the server you want, then tap the large Connect button.
<img src="/assets/ios/happ/11.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

5. You may be asked to allow the VPN connection. Select Allow, then enter your passcode.
<img src="/assets/ios/happ/12.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<img src="/assets/ios/happ/15.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

Congratulations, you are connected to the free internet! :)

:::tip

**✅ Tip**
If you tap the connect button but the VPN does not turn on, restart your phone once to fix the issue.
:::

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**
1. Do not keep another VPN connected while adding the update link.

2. Make sure your phone's time and date are set to automatic.

3. Do not copy any extra text with your subscription link. Copy only the subscription link.
:::
