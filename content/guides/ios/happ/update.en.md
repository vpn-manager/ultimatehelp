## Updating the Subscription Link

1. In the Happ app, tap the highlighted area shown in the image and wait a moment.

<img src="/assets/ios/happ/13.PNG"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">

2. If the update is successful, you will see the message shown in the image at the top of the screen.


:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while updating.
