## Sorting Servers by Ping

1. Tap the three dots in the top-right corner and select Real delay all configuration to run a ping test for all servers.

<img src="/assets/android/v2rayng/10.jpg"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

<img src="/assets/android/v2rayng/11.jpg"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

2. After the ping test finishes, tap the three dots in the top-right corner again and select Sorting by test results to sort the servers by the lowest ping.

<img src="/assets/android/v2rayng/13.jpg"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

<img src="/assets/android/v2rayng/12.jpg"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

Do not keep the VPN connected while sorting servers.
:::
