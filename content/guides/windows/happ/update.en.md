## Updating the Subscription Link

1. In the Happ app, click the highlighted area shown in the image and wait a moment.

<img src="/assets/windows/happ/9.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>


2. If the update is successful, you will see a success message at the top of the screen.

<img src="/assets/windows/happ/6.png"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:52%">
<br>

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

1. Do not keep the VPN connected while updating.

:::
