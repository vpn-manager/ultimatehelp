## Adding a Subscription Link

1. Open the bot and use the service management button to get your subscription link. Then copy the link.

2. In v2rayN, go to Subscription group and Subscription group setting, then select Add.

<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/51899bab-edfc-49ef-b5ad-6c0ac4d08c5f"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>

<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/3a53ac85-d7b4-4c91-8c62-f29fa2d8fb9c"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/d0f56dc2-5cf1-4b14-b3ff-50c73e4cc9b7"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>
3. In the window that opens, enter the required information.
For Remarks, enter any name you want. In this example, we entered `ok`.
For URL, paste the link you received from the bot in step 1.

<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/8dd37ae4-012c-4a0f-ae94-ab154b1aa583"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/d9b2d1b8-e10b-42e3-9686-8eb375e5aeba"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>
Then click Confirm.
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/04d4b433-0867-43e4-bb44-93587261a068"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>
4. On the main v2rayN page, open Subscription group again, then click update subscription without proxy.
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/13e1607f-dc85-444a-b824-ccb18d47e0a4"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>

5. After a short time, the servers will be added to the app list. To select a server, right-click the server you want and choose Set as active server.
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/5d322b3b-9cdd-430e-91cb-800d1b757d22"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>

6. Make sure the System proxy option at the bottom of the page is set to Set system proxy.
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/a89bff94-652b-40a8-bc1e-46f0652a58fc"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>


You can now use the free internet. :)

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**
1. Do not keep another VPN connected while adding the update link.

2. Make sure your time and date are set to automatic.
:::


## Updating the Subscription Link

1. On the main v2rayN page, open Subscription group again, then click update subscription without proxy.
<img src="https://github.com/VPNHELP/vpnhelp.github.io/assets/129318294/13e1607f-dc85-444a-b824-ccb18d47e0a4"
     style="display:block;float:none;margin-left:auto;margin-right:auto;width:81%">
<br>

2. After a short time, the server list will be updated.

:::warning

**⚠️ Note:**

Do not keep the VPN connected while updating servers.
:::
